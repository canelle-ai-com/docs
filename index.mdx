## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Sign in to Canelle AI and create a new project. Each project is a self-contained knowledge agent with its own content, settings, and deployment channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your knowledge">
    Upload PDFs, paste URLs, sync Notion or Confluence pages, or type notes directly. Canelle AI indexes everything automatically — no configuration needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your agent">
    Set a name, greeting message, language, and tone. Your agent answers only from what you've added, so configure it to match your brand and audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy anywhere">
    Embed a floating chat widget on your website, share a direct link, connect to Slack, or call the REST API. Your agent is live in seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
