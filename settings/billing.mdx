Your plan determines how many projects you can create, how much storage your knowledge bases can use, and how many AI credits you have available each month. Navigate to **Settings → Billing** at any time to review your current plan, monitor credit consumption, and manage payment details.

<Note>
  All plans include a 30-credit free trial. No credit card is required to start.
</Note>

## Plans Overview

| Plan | Price | Projects | Storage | AI Credits / mo | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | $9 / mo | 2 | 5 MB | 1,000 | Website widget, API access |
| **Professional** | $49 / mo | 5 | 25 MB | 5,000 | All channels, analytics, sentiment scoring |
| **Ultimate** | $129 / mo | Unlimited | 100 MB | 12,000 | Custom branding, SSO, priority support |

## AI Credits

Every message your agent processes consumes **1 AI credit**. Credits reset monthly on your billing renewal date — the same calendar day each month that you first subscribed. Unused credits do not roll over to the next period.

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Change Plan">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing** and click **Change plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your new plan">
    Review the plan options and click **Select** on the plan you want to upgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and enter payment details">
    Review the order summary. If you haven't added a payment method yet, enter your card details now, then click **Confirm upgrade**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your new plan immediately">
    Your upgraded plan and the full credit allowance for the new plan take effect right away. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period — you only pay for the days remaining, not the full month.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrades take effect at your **next renewal date**, not immediately. You keep your current plan's limits until then.

<Warning>
  If your current usage exceeds the lower plan's project or storage limits, Canelle AI will prompt you to reduce your projects or free up storage before the downgrade takes effect. Address these limits before your renewal date to avoid any disruption.
</Warning>

To initiate a downgrade, follow the same steps as upgrading: go to **Settings → Billing → Change plan**, select the lower-tier plan, and confirm.

## Invoices

Download a PDF copy of any past invoice from **Settings → Billing → Invoice history**. Each invoice lists the billing period, plan, credit allowance, any prorated charges, and the total amount charged.

## Payment Methods

Canelle AI accepts all major credit and debit cards. To update your payment method:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment method settings">
    Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment method**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new card">
    Click **Update payment method** and enter your new card details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save**. Your new card is used for all future charges. The previous card is removed automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Set up low-credit email notifications in **Settings → Profile → Notifications** so you're warned before your credits run out — giving you time to upgrade or adjust usage before your agent starts hitting its limit.
</Tip>
