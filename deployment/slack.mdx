The Slack integration lets your team query your knowledge agent without leaving Slack. Ask questions with a slash command and get answers posted back to the channel — keeping your team's workflow uninterrupted and your knowledge base just one command away.

<Warning>
  Slack integration is available on **Professional** and **Ultimate** plans. Upgrade your plan in **Settings → Billing** if you don't see the Slack option under Deploy.
</Warning>

## Requirements

Before you begin, confirm you have the following:

- A **Slack workspace admin** account — you need permission to install third-party apps into your workspace.
- A Canelle AI project on the **Professional or Ultimate** plan.

## Connect Canelle AI to Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Slack integration settings">
    In your project dashboard, navigate to **Deploy → Slack**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect to Slack">
    Click the **Connect to Slack** button. You'll be redirected to Slack's OAuth authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the Canelle AI app">
    Review the permissions the app requests, then click **Allow**. These permissions let the app read slash command invocations and post responses. Slack will redirect you back to your Canelle AI dashboard when authorization is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose which channels to enable">
    Select the specific channels where the agent should respond, or choose **All channels** to make it available workspace-wide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the installation">
    Click **Install**. The Canelle AI app is now active in your Slack workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using the agent in Slack

Once installed, any team member can query your agent using the `/canelle` slash command:

```
/canelle ask How do I reset my password?
```

The agent processes the question against your knowledge base and replies in a **threaded message** attached to your slash command — keeping the channel uncluttered while making the answer visible to everyone.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Slash command in a channel">
    Type the `/canelle ask` command followed by your question in any enabled channel. The response appears as a thread on your message.

    ```
    /canelle ask What are the steps to onboard a new employee?
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Direct message">
    Users can also open a **direct message with the Canelle AI app** for private, one-on-one questions that don't need to be visible to the rest of the channel.

    Search for **Canelle AI** in Slack's direct message search, then type your question naturally — no slash command needed in DMs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Manage the integration

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Disconnect Slack" icon="plug-circle-xmark">
    Go to **Deploy → Slack → Disconnect** to remove the Canelle AI app from your workspace. The slash command will stop responding immediately.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Change channel access" icon="sliders">
    Return to **Deploy → Slack** at any time to add or remove channels, or switch between specific channels and all-channels mode.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Each Slack message sent to your agent consumes AI credits from your plan. Monitor your credit usage in **Settings → Usage**. You'll receive an email alert when your credits fall below your configured threshold.
</Note>
