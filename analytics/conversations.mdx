The Analytics tab shows you how users are interacting with your agent — what they're asking, whether they're satisfied, and where the knowledge base has gaps. Use these insights to refine your content, close coverage gaps, and improve resolution rates over time.

## Accessing Analytics

Navigate to your project dashboard and select the **Analytics** tab in the top navigation bar.

## Overview Metrics

At the top of the Analytics page, four stat cards give you an at-a-glance summary of agent performance for the selected time period.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Conversations" icon="comments">
    The number of unique conversation sessions started by users. Each time a new user (or a returning user) begins a fresh session, it counts as one conversation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Messages" icon="message">
    The total number of individual messages sent by users. Every user message consumes one AI credit, so this figure directly maps to your credit consumption.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Resolution Rate" icon="circle-check">
    The percentage of conversations in which your agent provided a direct answer rather than triggering the fallback message. A higher resolution rate indicates stronger knowledge base coverage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avg. Response Sentiment" icon="face-smile">
    The average sentiment score across all conversations in the selected period. Scores range from **-1.0** (very negative) to **+1.0** (very positive), giving you a quick read on how users feel about their interactions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Conversation List

Below the overview cards, a full table lists every conversation recorded in the selected time period.

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Date** | Timestamp of the first message in the session |
| **First Message** | A short preview of the user's opening question |
| **Messages** | Total number of user messages in the session |
| **Sentiment** | Emoji indicator of the session's overall sentiment score |
| **Channel** | Where the conversation took place — Widget, Shared Link, Slack, or API |

Click any row to open the full conversation transcript, including every turn, your agent's responses, and any sources it cited.

## Unanswered Questions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Unanswered Questions tab">
    Select the **Unanswered Questions** tab next to the Conversations tab. This view lists every question where your agent fell back to the fallback message instead of providing a direct answer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review gaps in your knowledge base">
    Scan the list to identify recurring themes or high-frequency questions that your agent currently cannot answer. These represent the highest-impact opportunities to improve resolution rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content directly from the list">
    Click **Add to knowledge base** next to any question. Canelle AI creates a new text snippet pre-filled with that question as the title, ready for you to write a direct answer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Time Range Filter

Use the date picker in the top-right corner of the Analytics page to change the reporting window.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Preset Ranges">
    Choose from **Last 7 days**, **Last 30 days**, or **Last 90 days** for instant one-click filtering.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Custom Range">
    Select **Custom range** from the date picker, then choose a start and end date to analyse any specific period in your conversation history.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Exporting Data

On **Professional** and **Ultimate** plans, you can download your full conversation log for external analysis or record-keeping.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Analytics">
    Navigate to your project → **Analytics**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply your desired time range">
    Set the date picker to the period you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export CSV">
    Click the **Export CSV** button in the top-right corner. Your browser downloads a `.csv` file containing every conversation, message, sentiment score, and channel for the selected period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Review unanswered questions weekly and add content for the top 5 gaps. Even a short text snippet that directly answers a frequently asked but unanswered question can dramatically improve your resolution rate.
</Tip>
