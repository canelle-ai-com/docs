Canelle AI is an AI knowledge agent platform that transforms your existing content into a always-on assistant your team and customers can actually trust. Instead of relying on a general-purpose model that might guess or hallucinate, every answer Canelle AI delivers is grounded strictly in the documents, pages, and notes you choose to index — nothing more, nothing less.

## What Canelle AI Does

You connect your content sources — PDFs, websites, Notion pages, Confluence spaces, or plain text — and Canelle AI builds a searchable knowledge base from them. When someone asks a question, your AI agent retrieves the most relevant passages from that knowledge base and uses them to compose an accurate, cited response. If the answer isn't in your content, the agent says so instead of making something up.

## Who It's For

Canelle AI is built for teams who need reliable, self-service answers at scale without the overhead of maintaining a static FAQ or routing every question to a human.

- **Customer support teams** looking to deflect repetitive tickets with accurate, instant answers
- **Documentation owners** who want their docs to be conversational and instantly searchable
- **Internal teams** that need a 24/7 knowledge assistant for HR policies, onboarding guides, or engineering runbooks
